this is called project bigger fish

the core idea of the game is pretty simple, you live inside of a mech and go fish
rather than a standard mech battle game its a votv-like game, you manage your mech and fish by yourself or using the mech
- learn to control your mech for moving around a forested world, maybe theres a desert too
- learn to exit your mech, walk around on platforms outside of your mech, learn to clean and maintain your mech from the outside and the inside
- learn to go fishing by yourself, grow bait and such
- learn to fish using the mech too, this should probably be later

first goal is
- mech you can explore, both withing and on the outside
- cockpit with mech controls
